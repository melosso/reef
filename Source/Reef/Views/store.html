                How to use the Store
            

            
        

        

            
The Store walks you through setting up a known-good integration end to end - it creates and live-verifies every piece (Connection, Import, Template, Export Profile, and more) instead of leaving you to wire up Reef's data model manually.


            
Things to know:

            
    
                
  • Save & Verify: Each step saves your configuration, then runs a real live check (a real connection test, a real test email, a real template render) - not just a "saved to DB" checkmark.
    • 
                
  • Resume anytime: Closing the wizard mid-setup never deletes what you've created. Come back later and pick up exactly where you left off.
    • 
                
  • Optional steps: Some steps (like scheduling) can be skipped - you can always trigger things manually or come back later.
    • 
            


            

                
Tip: Every entity the wizard creates (Connections, Profiles, Destinations, etc.) is a normal Reef record - you can keep editing it from the regular pages after setup.
